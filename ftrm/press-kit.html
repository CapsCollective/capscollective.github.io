Brief Description


Fantasy Town Regional Manager roguelite, card-based town builder where you manage a fantasy town, dealing with the troubles of monsters, rowdy adventurers, and corporate oversight.



Development History


Fantasy Town Regional Manager originally started as a 3 month student project back in April of 2020, and with a little effort we're taking it to full release on Steam in October of 2021! The game is a town-building, collectible card gaming satire of the fantasy genre, asking the question "what's it like to run a fantasy town for adventurers?". Not great probably, but potentially quite fun!

It's a love-letter to role-playing games like D&D, and the oftentimes humorous and ridiculous situations that we've all experienced playing them.

We hope that people have much fun playing this game as we did making it!



About The Developers


We're a collective of university students and recent graduates from Sydney, Australia (mostly at UTS) with a shared love of game-making, running game jams, and building open-source software in our free time. Teams between projects often vary and it allows us to bring in whomever wants to be a part of whatever projects we're currently working on.

In late 2020, we decided to publish Fantasy Town Regional Manager under the name "Caps Collective", and we're working to deliver everything we do in as much of an open-source and transparent way possible!
